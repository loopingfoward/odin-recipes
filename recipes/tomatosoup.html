Tomato Soup

Home Page

Description

An ease Creamy Tomato Soup, comforting, and has a rich flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Chicken stock
  • Basil
  • Sugar
  • Black Pepper
  • Whipping cream
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Saute Aromatics
  2. Make the tomato soup base
  3. Blend if desired
  4. Add cream and Parmesan
  5. Serve
